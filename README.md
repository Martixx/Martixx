# Hi there, I'm Marta López Pardal (@Martixx) 👋

### Senior Cybersecurity Analyst | Incident Response Specialist | Tech Communicator

I work in defensive cybersecurity (Blue Team) as a Tier 3 Analyst and Incident Response Specialist. I help protect large-scale organizations by monitoring, detecting, and managing security incidents across highly demanding sectors. My goal is to ensure that incident response is fast and efficient, minimizing both impact and business disruption.

Alongside my technical role, I actively contribute to the cybersecurity ecosystem. I have served as a mentor for the Spanish Civil Guard's Cyberleague (*Ciberliga de la Guardia Civil*), deliver keynotes at technical conferences, and provide training across multiple Blue Team disciplines.


🛡️ **SOC Rule:** *“True defense-in-depth begins by inspecting what lies beneath the surface.”*
